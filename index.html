AI Scroll Navigator

Seamlessly jump through your Gemini and ChatGPT conversations.

The extension scans the page for your queries and creates a dynamic navigation bar on the right side of the screen.

  • 1

    Identify: It finds every message where the author is "User".

  • 2

    Generate: It creates a numbered dot for every query found.

  • 3

    Preview: Hover over a dot to see a tooltip with "Your Query: [Text Content]".

  • 4

    Navigate: Click a dot to scroll your query to the top of the window instantly.

  • 1
    Clone the repo to your device:
    Gemini: Git repo link
    ChatGPT: Git repo link
  • 2

    Open Chrome and type chrome://extensions/ in the address bar.

  • 3

    Enable Developer Mode in the top right corner.

  • 4

    Click Load unpacked and select your project folder.

  • 5

    Refresh your Gemini or ChatGPT tab and enjoy!
